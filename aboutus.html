About Me

Hi there! I'm Santra K, a passionate and creative web designer. I'm currently pursuing a degree in Computer Applications at Stella Maris College, Chennai, Tamil Nadu. I specialize in creating responsive, user-friendly, and visually stunning websites.

Ever since I first explored web design, I’ve been fascinated by the endless possibilities of creativity on the web. I believe in the power of design to communicate ideas clearly and engage users effectively. My goal is to create websites that are not only beautiful but also functional and easy to navigate.

I am proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and have hands-on experience with popular design tools such as Figma and GIMP. I focus on UI/UX principles to ensure the user’s experience is at the heart of my designs, making each project unique and tailored to the client’s needs.

Beyond academics, I love engaging in cultural events, content creation, and public speaking. Whether it's hosting events or designing innovative projects, I strive to make every experience impactful.

If you're interested in working together or want to learn more about my design approach, feel free to reach out—I’d love to collaborate with you!
